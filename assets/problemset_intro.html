Press 'Begin Practice' to start your practice, or 'Resume to' go to the problem that you last did in the previous session. To see your score for this problem set, press Review.

 

From within the problem, you will find many useful tools:

-       Use the Pen Tools with your fingers and use the Work Space as scratch paper to work out Math problems. Use the highlighter to mark important paragraphs and the red pen to underline key sentences in Reading Comprehension passages.

-       Take notes. Press the In Person button in the lower right (iPad only) to record your own voice and animation. Publish the recording for other people to share.

-       From within a problem, you can press Menu to access a couple useful features:

o   Bookmark problems that you want to revisit. Later you can access the bookmarked pages from the app’s main menu by pressing the bookmark icon in the upper left corner.

o   To email us your feedback, tap on Report a Problem.

 

Thank you for using our app and good luck with your preparation.
